## Marketing Destinations

Hardal offers various marketing destinations to help you connect your analytics data with your favorite marketing tools.

<Tip>
  The number of available destinations depends on your subscription tier:
  - **Basic**: 1 destination
  - **Professional**: 3 destinations
  - **Premium**: 5 destinations
  - **Enterprise**: Unlimited destinations
</Tip>
