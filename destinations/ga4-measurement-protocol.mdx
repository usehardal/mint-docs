## Overview

Send events to Google Analytics 4 using the Measurement Protocol.

## How to use

1. Go to your Hardal dashboard
2. Navigate to Marketing Destinations
3. Click "Add Custom Destination"
4. Select "GA4 Measurement Protocol" from templates
5. Configure the destination with your GA4 Measurement ID
6. Send events to GA4
